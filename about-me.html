About me

Hello, my name is Monserrat but you can call me Monse

I'm from the beautiful country of Mexico. I've been teaching Spanish for more than 5 years.

I had the opportunity to work on a ship, travel, and knowing amazing people from many different countries. This experience helped me to understand that although learning a new language is a challenge, the power to connect with people from different cultures is invaluable.

My teaching strategy is to develop every class focus on your way to learn, your goals, and the topics you are interested in. Don't worry if you don't know where to start, together we can find your learning path.

I look forward to knowing you and walk with you on this adventure of learning Spanish
